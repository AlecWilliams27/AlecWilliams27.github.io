I started my career in healthcare as a pharmacy professional and eventually worked my way up to Manager at BioPlus Specialty Pharmacy. That’s where I first discovered my love for data integration, validation, and reporting. My manager used to say, “Data doesn’t lie,” — and that phrase flipped a switch for me. What began as curiosity quickly became a passion for uncovering the stories behind the numbers and using data to make smarter, more human decisions.

I’m passionate about creating data-driven projects that are both insightful and beautifully designed. I have an eye for detail, a creative streak, and a genuine appreciation for the balance between art and analytics. I’ve always been a bit of an outlier — the one who sees things differently — but that perspective has shaped me into a resilient, adaptable leader who thrives on turning challenges into opportunities.

Right now, I’m looking for a career that lets me combine my technical skills and creative vision — a place where I can analyze, design, and communicate insights that truly make an impact. My goal is simple: to keep growing, keep learning, and keep building something greater than myself.

## 🌟 Strengths & Weaknesses Summary

I like to think of myself as equal parts curious scientist and chaotic organizer — I’ll experiment with a new idea, then build a spreadsheet to track the results before the coffee even kicks in. I’m big on structure, learning, and honest self-reflection (sometimes too honest). Feedback doesn’t scare me — I ask for it, take notes, and probably make a dashboard about it later.

On the flip side, patience isn’t exactly my strong suit — if progress had a loading bar, I’d be the one tapping the screen. I tend to take on more than I should because I genuinely believe I can “do it all,” and rest days still feel like an unsolved mystery. Oh, and perfectionism? Let’s just say I’ve “finalized” this paragraph at least four times already.
