This piece is all about duality — because yes, sunflowers symbolize positivity, warmth, and joy… but don’t get it twisted. 🌻 Just like in life, I can radiate good vibes all day long, but if you intentionally mess with that energy, you might catch the exact look you see here.

> It’s a reminder that even the most optimistic people have boundaries — and that protecting your peace is just as beautiful as spreading sunshine. Think of it as “I love you, but also… try me if you want.” 😄
