This piece was inspired by and dedicated to a very special friend and her beautiful family, who affectionately call me “Boo Bear.” The artwork channels the spirit of the bear — a powerful symbol of strength, courage, and introspection. It encourages us to explore our inner selves, find balance, and stand firm in our beliefs.

In times of need, I often embody this spirit: protective, nurturing, and resilient. The bear also represents healing and guardianship, guiding us to care deeply for others while remaining grounded in who we are. Through layered textures, expressive watercolor tones, and bold ink work, this piece captures that essence — a reflection of the strength and compassion that define both the bear and my own spirit.

> Here's to healing 🌟
