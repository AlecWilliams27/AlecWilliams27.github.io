This piece was created in the spirit of surrender and contentment — a visual expression of going inward to draw strength and continue on the path I was meant to walk. In moments when I felt disconnected from the world around me, introspection became my sanctuary, a safe haven from external chaos.

Through this work, I capture the stillness and power of self-reflection — the process of turning inward not as an escape, but as a way to evolve, heal, and realign with purpose. The figure’s meditative posture, surrounded by cosmic energy and blooming lotus flowers, symbolizes resilience, awakening, and the sacred journey of becoming.

## Cheers to my star seeds: may you always stay protected in your resilience. And when things grow dark, remember the power and light you carry — it is precious beyond measure.
