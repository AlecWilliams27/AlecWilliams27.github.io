This pencil and watercolor drawing captures a precious moment between my sister and my niece — a bond that’s pure, gentle, and deeply rooted in love. Through every soft line and delicate shade, I wanted to reflect the warmth, strength, and tenderness that exists between them. It’s a simple yet meaningful reminder of the beauty found in family and the love that shapes us from the very beginning.
