This mixed-media portrait blends traditional and futuristic aesthetics to explore the duality between humanity and technology. Created using pencil for structure, marker for bold definition, and watercolor for tonal depth, the piece captures a striking face that transitions into a cybernetic form.

Half of the face retains its natural human features — expressive eyes and soft shading that evoke emotion and identity. The other half is transformed by mechanical precision: metallic plating, circuitry, and a glowing red eye that suggests inner energy or consciousness. The combination of grayscale and selective red accents emphasizes contrast between organic life and artificial intelligence.

> The smooth watercolor gradients bring warmth and depth, while the marker outlines and pencil details create texture and balance. This piece invites viewers to question where the human ends and the machine begins — a symbolic look at evolution, resilience, and the merging of creativity with technology.
