> “Eye Rose” – Self-Portrait by Alec Williams

Created with colored pencil, watercolor, and acrylic, this self-portrait is a deeply personal expression of the depression I’ve endured and the growth that followed. The rose symbolizes spiritual awakening, vulnerability, and the breakthroughs that come from pain — a reminder that even through darkness, insight and transformation can bloom from within.
