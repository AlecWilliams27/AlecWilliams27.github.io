# 📌 Project Overview
I was tasked with analyzing data from Zomato and building a dashboard that not only visualized the data but also provided clear conclusions and recommendations.

The project consisted of two major parts: *Decomposition (Research Plan) → Breaking down the business problem into a structured plan of analysis. *Dashboard & Report → Building an interactive dashboard to present findings and delivering recommendations in a report format.

🌟 Please click the links at the bottom of this page to review the live dashboard and decompostion 🌟

## 🛠️ Project Workflow

### Step 1: Understanding the Business Problem
* I received a business case with multiple possible research directions.
* I chose my focus area after carefully reviewing the data and goals.
* My plan (decomposition) outlined the metrics, hypotheses, and visualizations I would use.

### Step 2: Decomposition (Research Plan)
My decomposition served as the blueprint for my analysis and included:

* Key Questions → What I wanted to learn from the data.
* Hypotheses → Assumptions about customer behavior, sales, or restaurant performance.
* Data Preparation Steps → How I planned to clean and organize the data.
* Planned Visualizations → The types of charts and dashboards I would use to answer each question.

This structured plan ensured my reviewer understood exactly what would be delivered in the final dashboard. 

### Step 3: Dashboard Development 
I created an interactive dashboard in Power BI. Each visualization told part of a larger story about restaurant performance and customer activity. The dashboard combined KPIs, charts, and filters to make the insights clear and actionable. I made sure every visualization was tied to the research area I selected.

### Step 4: Report & Recommendations 
Alongside the dashboard, I prepared a report summarizing my findings. The report highlighted conclusions drawn from the data and business recommendations that Zomato could act on. This was the most important part of the project because analysis without recommendations would not meet the project requirements.

 💡 Key Takeaways A successful project goes beyond just building visuals — it requires storytelling, structured planning, and actionable recommendations. My Zomato dashboard showcased not only data analysis but also business strategy insights, making it valuable for decision-making.

## ✨ This project demonstrates my ability to plan, analyze, visualize, and communicate insights effectively—end-to-end analytics from decomposition to recommendations.
