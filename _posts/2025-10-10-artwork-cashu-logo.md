“Cashu World” is more than just graffiti — it’s a piece of my story. Back in my teenage years, when life felt heavy and reality was hard to face, graffiti became my escape. The bold colors and playful style you see here represent the freedom I found in creating something that was completely my own. Cashu was a name given to me by one of the greatest friends I ever had — someone who believed in me, pushed me out of my comfort zone, and helped me see my own potential when I couldn’t see it myself. This piece is dedicated to them. 

## Rest in peace, Tech Trackz — your memory lives on in every step I take. And even now, when the rain falls, I still find myself dancing in it, just like we used to.
